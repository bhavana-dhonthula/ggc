ABOUT

The Department of Commerce at GIRRAJ GOVERNMENT College is a vibrant and dynamic department that offers a comprehensive range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in commerce. The department is committed to providing its students with a strong theoretical and practical foundation in the field of commerce, preparing them for successful careers in the industry.

Faculty

The department boasts a team of highly qualified and experienced faculty members who are dedicated to teaching and research. The faculty members have a strong track record of academic excellence and are actively involved in industry and professional activities.

Programs

The department offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in commerce. The undergraduate programs include Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com.), Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA). The postgraduate programs include Master of Commerce (M.Com.), Master of Business Administration (MBA).

Facilities

The department has state-of-the-art facilities to support its teaching and learning activities. These facilities include well-equipped classrooms, computer labs, and a library. The department also has a strong network of industry partners who provide students with opportunities for internships and placements.

Placements

The department has a strong track record of placements. The department's alumni are employed in a variety of industries, including banking, finance, insurance, consulting, and retail.

Student Activities

The department encourages students to participate in a variety of extracurricular activities. These activities include seminars.
